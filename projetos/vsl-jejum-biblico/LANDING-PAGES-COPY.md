## 📊 Landing Page Strategy

**3 Landing Pages (different use cases):**

1. **Pre-VSL:** Lead em + e-mail → oferece acesso ao VSL
2. **Post-VSL:** Pessoa asskissed assiste VSL → pré-registro pro próxima turma
3. **Ads:** Short-form (headlines + 1-2 linhas) para ads pagos

**Conversion Metrics Target:**
- Pre-VSL: 40-50% email capture rate (baixa fricção)
- Post-VSL: 15-25% pre-register rate (mais alta fricção)
- Ads: 2-5% click-through rate (benchmark pago)

## 🎨 DESIGN SPECIFICATIONS

### Color Palette (All Landing Pages)

**Primary Colors:**
- Warm Gold: #DAA520 (trust, spirituality)
- Deep Blue: #2C3E50 (credibility, calm)
- Warm Beige: #F5E6D3 (approachable, safe)

**Accent Colors:**
- Green: #27AE60 (growth, transformation)
- Soft Orange: #E67E22 (energy, hope)

**Text Colors:**
- Headlines: #2C3E50 (deep blue)
- Body: #34495E (dark gray)
- Links: #E67E22 (soft orange)

### Typography

**Headlines (H1, H2):** Serif (Georgia, Garamond) — classic, spiritual  
**Body Text:** Sans-serif (Helvetica, Open Sans) — clean, modern  
**Buttons:** Bold sans-serif

### Layout  

**Desktop:**
- 1200px max-width
- Padding: 40px sides
- Sections: Full-width with alternating background colors

**Mobile:**
- 100% width (responsive)
- Touch-friendly buttons (50px height minimum)
- Vertical stack (no columns for small screens)

### Imagery

**Pre-VSL:**
- Hero image: Pessoa em paz (meditação, natureza, oração)
- Color treatment: Warm filter (dourado/laranja)
- Quality: High-res, authentic (not too "stock photo")

**Post-VSL:**
- Testimonial photos: Real people (not models)
- Community images: Groups, circles, support vibes

**Favicon/Logo:**
- Simple cross + flame = spiritual transformation
- Colors: Gold/Blue combo

## 🔧 TECHNICAL IMPLEMENTATION

### Recommended Platform

**Option 1: Webflow (Recommended)**
- Drag/drop design
- Built-in form handling
- E-mail integrations (Zapier)
- Mobile-responsive ready
- Cost: $12-36/mo

**Option 2: ConvertKit / Leadpages (Simpler)**
- Pre-made templates (VSL optimized)
- Built-in email/form workflows
- One-click integrations
- Cost: $29-99/mo

**Option 3: Unbounce (Most Optimized)**
- A/B testing built-in
- Advanced analytics
- Integration with drip campaigns
- Cost: $75-300/mo

### Email Integration Workflow

```
User submits form → Zapier webhook triggers 
→ Email sent (Welcome email with VSL link)
→ Automatically added to email list
→ Sequences:
   Day 1: Welcome + VSL link
   Day 3: Reminder to watch
   Day 5: Post-VSL: "Ready to join?"
   Day 7: Last chance for pre-reg
```
