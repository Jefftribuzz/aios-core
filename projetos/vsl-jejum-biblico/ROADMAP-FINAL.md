## ⚠️ BIGGEST RISKS & MITIGATION

### Risk 1: "Não conseguir encontrar depoimentos reais a tempo"
**Impact:** HIGH — Delays production, reduces authenticity  
**Probability:** MEDIUM  
**Mitigation:**
- Usar rede pastores/padres (insider knowledge)
- Oferecer pequeno incentivo (R$ 100-200)
- Prepare "composite testimonial" como fallback
- Start search IMEDIATO (não delay)

### Risk 2: "Video production caro demais / budget rejected"
**Impact:** CRITICAL — Bloqueia toda Fase 4  
**Probability:** MEDIUM  
**Mitigation:**
- Use parte do orçamento pra "simple setup" (phone camera)
- Negocie discount com producer (package deal)
- Split em 2 rodadas de filmagem (reduce daily cost)
- Fundraise se necessário

### Risk 3: "Landing page conversion rate baixa"
**Impact:** MEDIUM — Affects email list building  
**Probability:** MEDIUM  
**Mitigation:**
- A/B test agressivamente (headlines, CTAs, forms)
- Use heat mapping (Hotjar) pra ver onde users drop off
- Leverage social proof (real testimonials)
- Optimize mobile experience agressively

### Risk 4: "Ads não performam ou high CPL (cost per lead)"
**Impact:** MEDIUM — Affects ROI  
**Probability:** MEDIUM  
**Mitigation:**
- Start with small budget (R$ 1,000-2,000) test
- Target lookalike audiences (warm)
- Use video ads (higher engagement VSL)
- Continuously optimize based on daily data

### Risk 5: "Team burnout (6 months is long project)"
**Impact:** MEDIUM — Quality suffers  
**Probability:** LOW-MEDIUM  
**Mitigation:**
- Clear sprints + 1-week breaks
- Celebrate small wins
- Delegate heavily (trusted contractors)
- Realistic timelines (don't over-commit)
